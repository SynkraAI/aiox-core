# 📝 Template: Day in the Life

## Overview

**Type:** Behind the Scenes / Lifestyle
**Avg Views:** 500K-2M
**Best For:** Building connection, showing authenticity, aspirational content

## Structure

```
[0-3s] HOOK - Pattern Interrupt
"Você não acredita no que acontece no meu dia..."
Visual: Unexpected morning scene OR dramatic moment
Audio: Alarm sound OR trending audio

[3-8s] MORNING ROUTINE
"5:30am: [unique morning habit]"
Visual: Time-lapse or quick cuts
Audio: Upbeat, energetic

[8-20s] WORK/HUSTLE MONTAGE
"Aqui é onde a mágica acontece..."
- Quick cut 1: Main work activity
- Quick cut 2: Behind the scenes
- Quick cut 3: Struggle moment (relatable)
Visual: Dynamic transitions, B-roll
Audio: Building momentum

[20-35s] HIGHLIGHT MOMENT
"Esse foi o momento que fez tudo valer a pena:"
[Show the payoff - meeting, achievement, creation]
Visual: Slow-mo for impact
Audio: Climax moment

[35-50s] REAL TALK - Authenticity
"O que ninguém mostra: [struggle/honest moment]"
Visual: Raw, unfiltered footage
Audio: Softer, personal tone

[50-55s] EVENING WIND-DOWN
"E assim termina mais um dia..."
Visual: Sunset, relaxation, family time

[55-60s] CTA
"Quer ver como foi [amanhã/próxima semana]? Me segue!"
Visual: Follow button animation
```

## Visual Execution

### Shot Types Needed

1. **Wake-up shot** - Alarm, first moment
2. **Routine B-roll** - Coffee, setup, commute
3. **Work montage** - 3-5 quick cuts of tasks
4. **Highlight shot** - The payoff moment
5. **Vulnerability shot** - Real, unfiltered
6. **Wind-down shot** - Evening, reflection

### Transitions

- Clock wipe (time passing)
- Match cuts (same action, different time)
- Speed ramps (energy shifts)

## Pro Tips

1. **Show the struggle** - Perfect days don't connect
2. **One highlight > many mediocre moments**
3. **End with curiosity** for next video
4. **Use trending audio** for algorithm boost
5. **Film B-roll constantly** - You'll need it
