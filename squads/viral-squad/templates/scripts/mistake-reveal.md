# ⚠️ Template: Mistake Reveal

## Structure

```
[0-3s] HOOK
"STOP doing [common mistake]. You're wasting [time/money/effort]."
Visual: Red X over mistake, dramatic

[3-10s] AGITATION
"Everyone does this. But it's killing your [results/progress]."
Visual: Show negative consequences

[10-40s] EDUCATION
"Here's why it doesn't work: [science/reason]
And here's what actually works: [solution with proof]"
Visual: Side-by-side comparison

[40-55s] PAYOFF
"I did this for years until I learned [key insight]"
Visual: Personal transformation

[55-60s] CTA
"Tag someone who needs to hear this 👇"
Visual: Share prompt with urgency
```

## Best For

- Educational content
- Fitness/Health niches
- Business/Productivity
- Contrarian views

## Performance

- Controversy = High engagement
- Share rate: 3-5% (tag CTA)
- Comment rate: 2-4% (debate)
