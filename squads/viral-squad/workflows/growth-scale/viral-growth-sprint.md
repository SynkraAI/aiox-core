Crescer 10K-100K seguidores em 4 semanas através de conteúdo viral

## Timeline

**Duração:** 4 semanas

- **@viral-topics** (ideias)
- **@trend-hunter** (trends)

## SEMANA 2: Production Sprint

### Dia 8-9: Batch Recording

```
RECORD:
- 7-8 videos
- Multiple takes
- B-roll footage
- Thumbnail shots

TIPS:
- Same lighting setup
- Batch similar formats
- Energy high throughout
```

### Dia 10-12: Editing Sprint

```
@remotion-architect + @animation-pro

EDIT:
- All 7-8 videos
- Apply design system
- Add animations
- Audio sync

OUTPUT:
- 7-8 videos ready
```

### Dia 13-14: Publishing Week 2

```
SCHEDULE:
- 1-2 posts/day
- Optimal times (7am, 7pm)
- Stagger for reach

ENGAGE:
- Reply ALL comments (30 min)
- Stories with polls
- DM engaged followers
```

**DAILY:** Monitor metrics, adjust if needed
**DELIVERABLE:** 7-8 videos published
