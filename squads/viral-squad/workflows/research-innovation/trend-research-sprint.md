# 🔍 Trend Research Sprint Workflow

## Objetivo

Pesquisa profunda de trends virais para identificar oportunidades emergentes antes dos competitors, garantindo first-mover advantage.

**Tempo:** 1 semana
**Output:** Trend report + 10+ content ideas + timing strategy

---

## Quando Usar

- Quer se antecipar ao mercado (not react)
- Precisa pipeline de content ideas (30+ dias)
- Competitor está dominando (need new angle)
- Content feels stale (need refresh)
- Quarterly planning (strategic)

---

## Trend Lifecycle

```
TREND STAGES:

1. UNDERGROUND (0-2 semanas)
   - Early adopters testing
   - Low competition
   - High risk / High reward
   - Best ROI se pegar

2. EMERGING (2-6 semanas)
   - Growing fast
   - Moderate competition
   - Good risk/reward balance
   - SWEET SPOT ✅

3. MAINSTREAM (6-12 semanas)
   - Peak popularity
   - High competition
   - Lower differentiation
   - Still viable (if executed well)

4. DECLINING (12+ semanas)
   - Oversaturated
   - Audience fatigue
   - Low ROI
   - AVOID ❌
```

## Fase 2: Trend Validation (1-2 dias)

### 2.1 Data Analysis

**@metrics-guru + @trend-hunter:**

```markdown
TREND SCORING FRAMEWORK:

For Each Trend Identified (10-15):

Score Criteria (1-10 each):

1. GROWTH VELOCITY
   - How fast is it growing?
   - TikTok → Instagram lag (7-14 dias)
   - Score 1-10

2. COMPETITION LEVEL
   - How many creators using it?
   - Can we differentiate?
   - Score 1-10 (inverse: less competition = higher score)

3. AUDIENCE RELEVANCE
   - Fits our niche?
   - Audience expressed interest?
   - Score 1-10

4. LONGEVITY POTENTIAL
   - Flash in pan or sustained?
   - Cultural staying power?
   - Score 1-10

5. EXECUTION FEASIBILITY
   - Can we execute well?
   - Resources required?
   - Score 1-10

6. VIRAL POTENTIAL
   - Shareable?
   - Emotional trigger?
   - Score 1-10

TOTAL SCORE: Sum / 60 × 100 = %

TARGET: >70% to pursue
```

**Example Scoring:**

```markdown
TREND: "Day in Life of [profession]" format

1. Growth Velocity: 9/10
   - TikTok: 2.1B views (7 dias)
   - Instagram: 180M views (emerging)

2. Competition: 7/10
   - Moderate competition
   - Niche-specific angle possible

3. Audience Relevance: 8/10
   - Fits our niche (business/productivity)
   - Audience poll: 78% interested

4. Longevity: 7/10
   - Format sustainable
   - Not flash-in-pan

5. Execution Feasibility: 9/10
   - Low production cost
   - Authenticity matters more

6. Viral Potential: 8/10
   - Curiosity-driven
   - Behind-the-scenes appeal

TOTAL: 48/60 = 80% ✅ PURSUE
```

## Pro Tips

### Do's ✅

- Scan daily (trends move fast)
- Validate with data (not gut feel)
- Act on emerging (not mainstream)
- Batch produce (efficiency)
- Monitor performance (iterate)

### Don'ts ❌

- Não chase every trend (focus)
- Não wait too long (miss window)
- Não ignore audience demand (validation)
- Não batch post (algorithm penalty)
- Não forget to document learnings
