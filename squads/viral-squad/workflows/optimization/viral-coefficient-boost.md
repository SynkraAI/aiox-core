# 📊 Viral Coefficient Boost Workflow

## Objetivo

Aumentar viral coefficient (compartilhamentos/views) de <1.5 para >3.0 através de otimizações estratégicas de share triggers.

**Tempo:** 2 dias
**Output:** Vídeo otimizado + 2x-3x mais compartilhamentos

---

## Quando Usar

- Viral coefficient <1.5 (low sharing)
- Views altos mas poucos shares
- Quer explosão orgânica
- Need algorithm boost via shares

---

## Viral Coefficient Explicado

```
Viral Coefficient = (Shares / Views) × 100

<1.0 = Not viral (decaying)
1.0-1.5 = Steady (maintaining)
1.5-3.0 = Viral (growing)
>3.0 = Mega Viral (exponential)

Example:
1M views, 15K shares = 1.5 coef (viral)
1M views, 30K shares = 3.0 coef (mega viral)
```

## Fase 2: Share Trigger Optimization (4-6 horas)

### 2.1 Identificar Share Triggers

**@viral + @engagement-engineer:**

```markdown
7 SHARE TRIGGERS (STEPPS Framework):

1. SOCIAL CURRENCY (make them look smart/cool)
   - "Share this to help others"
   - "People need to see this"

2. TRIGGERS (top of mind = tip of tongue)
   - Tie to daily situation
   - "Next time you [X], remember this"

3. EMOTION (high arousal emotions)
   - Awe, anger, anxiety, excitement
   - NOT sadness (low arousal)

4. PUBLIC (observable, viral-worthy)
   - "Tag someone who needs this"
   - "Send this to your [X] friend"

5. PRACTICAL VALUE (useful, save-worthy)
   - Already strong (8.2% save rate)
   - Maintain

6. STORIES (narrative, relatable)
   - Personal transformation
   - "This changed my [X]"

7. CONTROVERSY (debate-worthy)
   - Strong opinion
   - "Hot take: [X]"
```

## Pro Tips

### Do's ✅

- Use multiple share triggers (3-5)
- Maintain retention durante edits
- A/B test sempre antes scale
- Monitor first 2h meticulosamente
- Create share trigger library

### Don'ts ❌

- Não sacrifice retention por shares
- Não pedir shares explicitamente ("share this")
- Não spam cross-platform
- Não ignore caption power (40% decision)
- Não escale sem validação
