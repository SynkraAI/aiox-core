## Growth Phases Framework

### Phase 1: Foundation (0-5K followers)

```yaml
Focus: Niche establishment & consistency

Posting:
  Frequency: 1-2x per day
  Best times: Test and find YOUR best times
  Content mix: 80% niche, 20% personality

Strategy:
  - Hyper-specific niche content
  - Engage with every comment
  - Comment on larger accounts (value-add, not spam)
  - Build small but engaged community
  - Focus on save rate over views

Goals:
  - Weekly growth: 500-1K
  - Save rate: >5
  - Comment rate: >2

Avoid:
  - Trying to go viral (consistency first)
  - Broad content (niche down)
  - Buying followers (algorithm poison)
```

### Phase 2: Acceleration (5K-50K followers)

```yaml
Focus: Viral attempts & collaborations

Posting:
  Frequency: 5-10x per week
  Best times: Data-driven from Phase 1
  Content mix: 60% proven, 40% experimental

Strategy:
  - Attempt viral content (trend riding)
  - Collaborations with similar-size creators
  - Series content (builds anticipation)
  - Cross-platform distribution
  - Email list building starts

Goals:
  - Weekly growth: 2-5K
  - Viral attempts: 1-2 per week
  - Collaboration: 1-2 per month

Tactics:
  - Duets/stitches with larger creators
  - Trend jacking within niche
  - Controversy (calculated)
  - Before/after transformations
```

### Phase 3: Scale (50K+ followers)

```yaml
Focus: Content machine & monetization

Posting:
  Frequency: 3-7x per week (quality > quantity)
  Best times: Peak engagement windows
  Content mix: 50% proven, 30% series, 20% experimental

  - Weekly growth: 5-10K+

Tactics:

  - Lookalike audience targeting

  - Product launches
```

## Viral Distribution Playbook

### Organic Distribution (First 24 hours)

```yaml
Immediate (0-5 minutes): ✅ Share to Stories (add poll/question)
  ✅ DM to 10 most engaged followers
  ✅ Post in close friends first

First 30 minutes: ✅ Reply to EVERY comment (algorithm signal)
  ✅ Like all comments
  ✅ Heart comments
  ✅ Ask follow-up questions in replies

First 2 hours: ✅ Second wave of engagement
  ✅ Pin best comment
  ✅ Share to Stories again (if allowed)
  ✅ Engage with your feed

First 24 hours: ✅ Continue replying
  ✅ Cross-post to other platforms
  ✅ Analyze initial metrics
  ✅ Decide on amplification
```

### Viral Detection (When to Amplify)

```yaml
Signs of Viral Potential:
  Views: 2x+ your average in first hour
  Save rate: >8
  Share rate: >3
  Comments: Higher than usual

If Viral Signs Detected: 1. DON'T change anything
  2. DON'T edit caption
  3. Let algorithm work
  4. Maximize engagement in comments
  5. Plan follow-up content

If MEGA Viral (100K+ in 24h): 1. Post follow-up within 24-48h
  2. Create series around topic
  3. Consider paid amplification
  4. Capture audience (bio link)
```

### Paid Amplification Strategy

```yaml
When to Boost:
  - Save rate >8% (proven content)
  - Organic performance above average
  - Strategic content (launch, funnel)

Budget Allocation:
  Test: $50-100 (validate audience response)
  Scale: $200-500 (if test performs)
  Push: $1000+ (confirmed winner)

Targeting:
  - Lookalike: Your engaged followers
  - Interest: Niche-specific
  - Behavior: Engaged with similar content

Metrics to Watch:
  - Cost per follower: <$1 is good
  - Cost per engagement: <$0.10
  - ROI: Track downstream conversion
```

## Viral Coefficient Optimization

### Understanding Viral Coefficient (K)

```yaml
Formula:
  K = i × c
  Where:
    i = invitations per user (shares per viewer)
    c = conversion rate (% who follow from share)

Interpretation:
  K < 1: Declining (not sustainable)
  K = 1: Stable (maintenance mode)
  K > 1: Growing (viral)
  K > 2: Explosive growth

Target: K > 1.5 for sustainable viral growth
```

### Improving K Factor

```yaml
Increase Shares (i):
  - Create "tag a friend" moments
  - Make content share-worthy (relatable)
  - Include share triggers
  - Create FOMO moments
  - Controversial (but not toxic)

Increase Conversion (c):
  - Strong profile (clear value prop)
  - Consistent content quality
  - Clear niche (know what to expect)
  - CTA in content ("follow for more")
  - Bio optimization
```
