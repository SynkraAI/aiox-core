# Niche Validation Checklist

> Validate market viability before committing resources to a new affiliate niche.

**Fase:** Pesquisa
**Usado por:** `workflows/niche-research.md`, `tasks/validate-niche.md`
**Items:** 8

---

## Checklist

- [ ] **Search demand >= 1K/mo** — Verify primary keyword(s) have at least 1,000 monthly searches via Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Keyword Planner; document exact volume and tool used
- [ ] **Commission rate >= 15%** — Confirm affiliate program(s) offer minimum 15% commission or equivalent CPL/CPA that meets target ROI; document rate and cookie window
- [ ] **Competition DR < 50** — Analyze top 10 SERP results for main keyword; majority of ranking pages must come from domains with DR below 50
- [ ] **Min 3 affiliate programs** — Identify at least 3 independent affiliate programs in the niche to avoid single-program dependency; list names, networks, and payout terms
- [ ] **Seasonality mapped** — Run Google Trends for 5-year view; document peak/off-peak months and determine if off-season revenue is acceptable
- [ ] **KGR < 0.25 keywords found** — Identify at least 5 keywords with Keyword Golden Ratio (allintitle results / monthly volume) below 0.25 for initial ranking opportunities
- [ ] **TAM/SAM/SOM estimated** — Document Total Addressable Market, Serviceable Addressable Market, and Serviceable Obtainable Market with data sources and assumptions
- [ ] **GO/NO-GO decision documented** — Record final decision with rationale in `data/niche-research/` folder; all 7 items above must be checked OR explicit exception noted with justification
