# Social Content Checklist

> Ensure each social media post meets platform standards, compliance requirements, and engagement best practices.

**Fase:** Social
**Usado por:** `workflows/social-publish.md`, `tasks/social-content-review.md`
**Items:** 9

---

## Checklist

- [ ] **Hook in first 3 seconds** — Video or carousel first frame, or first line of text, delivers a compelling hook that creates curiosity, states a problem, or makes a bold claim; tested against platform preview (muted, thumbnail)
- [ ] **Platform-native format (not cross-posted)** — Content is created or adapted specifically for the target platform's format and culture; not a direct cross-post from another platform without modification (e.g., TikTok watermark removed for Reels)
- [ ] **Affiliate disclosure in bio/caption** — "#ad", "#parceria", "#publi", or equivalent platform-appropriate disclosure appears at the beginning or clearly visible in the caption; disclosure is in bio if link-in-bio leads to affiliate offer
- [ ] **CTA clear and specific** — Call to action is explicit and tells the viewer exactly what to do next ("Link na bio", "Salva esse post", "Comenta SIM"); one primary CTA per post, not multiple competing actions
- [ ] **Hashtags researched (5-15 per post)** — Hashtag set includes a mix of high-volume (>500K posts), mid-volume (10K-500K), and niche-specific (<10K) tags relevant to the content; researched via platform search, not generic lists
- [ ] **Posting at optimal time for platform** — Post scheduled during peak engagement window for the specific account's audience (check platform analytics); not posted at arbitrary times
- [ ] **Content ratio 3:1 value:pitch** — For every promotional/affiliate post in the content calendar, at least 3 value-only posts (educational, entertaining, or inspirational) are published; ratio tracked in content calendar
- [ ] **Visual quality meets platform standards** — Images and videos meet platform minimum resolution requirements; no watermarks from competing platforms; brand colors and fonts consistent with account identity
- [ ] **Link in bio updated** — If post references "link na bio" or equivalent, the bio link has been updated to the correct destination before publishing; link resolves correctly on mobile
