# Prop Trading Content Checklist

> Ensure all prop trading affiliate content meets regulatory, ethical, and disclosure requirements before publishing.

**Fase:** Prop Trading
**Usado por:** `workflows/prop-content-publish.md`, `tasks/prop-content-review.md`
**Items:** 8

---

## Checklist

- [ ] **Risk disclaimer 12pt+ font visible** — A clear risk warning is displayed in a minimum 12-point font (or equivalent visual prominence) on every page promoting prop trading firms; disclaimer must be visible without scrolling on desktop
- [ ] **"Not financial advice" disclosure** — Explicit "not financial advice" or "purely informational" disclosure is present on all content; disclosure appears near the top of the page, not only in footer or terms of service
- [ ] **Affiliate relationship disclosed** — Affiliate or referral relationship with the prop firm is clearly disclosed to readers before any promotional content; disclosure uses plain language (e.g., "I earn a commission if you sign up through my link")
- [ ] **No guaranteed profits language** — Content contains no statements that guarantee, promise, or strongly imply consistent profits from prop trading; phrases like "make money guaranteed", "earn daily", or "risk-free profit" are absent
- [ ] **No "easy money" implications** — Content does not imply that passing prop firm challenges or generating profits is easy, quick, or requires minimal effort; realistic pass rates and failure rates are referenced where known
- [ ] **Past performance disclaimer** — Any reference to trader results, backtests, or historical data includes a disclaimer that past performance does not guarantee future results; specific results include full context (timeframe, market conditions, account size)
- [ ] **Regulatory status of firm mentioned** — Content references whether the prop firm is regulated, unregulated, or operates under a specific legal structure; readers are informed to conduct their own due diligence on the firm's legitimacy
- [ ] **All claims factual and verifiable** — Every specific claim (payout percentages, challenge parameters, profit splits, account sizes) is verifiable against current official firm documentation; no outdated or unverified promotional claims
