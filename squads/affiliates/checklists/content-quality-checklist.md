# Content Quality Checklist

> Ensure every piece of content meets E-E-A-T standards and provides genuine value beyond affiliate promotion.

**Fase:** SEO/Content
**Usado por:** `workflows/content-publish.md`, `tasks/content-review.md`
**Items:** 8

---

## Checklist

- [ ] **First-hand experience demonstrated** — Content includes specific details that prove the author has personally used, tested, or researched the product/service; generic or AI-only content without experiential evidence fails this check
- [ ] **Original data or testing included** — At least one element of original research, test result, screenshot, data point, or proprietary finding that cannot be found on other sites
- [ ] **Author entity established** — Author has a complete profile page, verifiable online presence (LinkedIn, professional site, or social), and consistent byline across published content
- [ ] **E-E-A-T signals present** — Content demonstrates Experience (used the product), Expertise (domain knowledge), Authoritativeness (recognized in field), and Trustworthiness (cites sources, discloses affiliations)
- [ ] **No thin affiliate content** — Every page provides substantial standalone value beyond the affiliate link; no pages whose only purpose is to redirect visitors without meaningful content
- [ ] **Helpful to user (not just SEO)** — Content answers the searcher's actual question completely; passes the "would this be recommended to a friend?" test; not written primarily to rank
- [ ] **Updated within 6 months** — Publication or last-updated date is within 6 months; if content is older, a fresh review and update has been performed and "Updated:" date reflects this
- [ ] **Factual claims sourced** — All statistics, study references, and product specifications link to primary sources; no unverified superlatives ("best", "only", "#1") without substantiation
