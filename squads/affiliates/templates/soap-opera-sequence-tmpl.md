## How Soap Opera Sequences Work

Each email ends with an **open loop** — a cliffhanger that compels the reader to open the next email. The arc builds toward the affiliate offer as the natural resolution of the story. Chaperon's method: real story → real emotions → real offer.

## A/B Test Ideas

- E1 subject: benefit-lead vs. curiosity-lead
- E3 subject: name the product vs. describe the concept
- E5: deadline in subject vs. "I almost didn't send this"
- CTA button text: "Check it out" vs. "Get [Product] Now"
