## Variant Guide

**COLD TRAFFIC variant** (from paid ads, social, organic):
- Longer story arc
- More problem agitation
- Stronger social proof
- Softer CTA language ("Discover" not "Buy Now")

**WARM TRAFFIC variant** (from email list, retargeting):
- Shorter setup
- Skip to discovery/results faster
- Can use harder CTA language
- Less social proof needed (trust already established)

## Page Production Notes

- **Above the fold:** Headline + sub-headline must load instantly, no slow hero images
- **Page length:** Cold = 600–1200 words | Warm = 300–600 words
- **CTA placement:** After results section + bottom of page (cold) | After product intro + bottom (warm)
- **Mobile:** Single column, CTA button full width, 16px+ body text
- **No navigation:** Remove header/footer nav to reduce exit points
