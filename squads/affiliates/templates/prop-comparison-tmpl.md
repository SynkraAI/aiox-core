## Methodology

We evaluate prop trading firms on the following criteria:
1. Rule fairness and clarity
2. Fee structure vs. funding level
3. Payout process and speed
4. Platform quality and reliability
5. Support responsiveness
6. Community and trader feedback
7. Track record and longevity

We do not rank firms based on affiliate commission rate. Firms without affiliate programs may outrank firms with affiliate programs if their offering is superior.
