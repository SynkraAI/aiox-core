## Series Overview

| # | Day | Type | Goal | CTA |
|---|-----|------|------|-----|
| 1 | 0 | Welcome + Deliver | Deliver lead magnet, set expectations | Lead magnet download |
| 2 | 1 | Story / Why | Build personal connection | None (relationship) |
| 3 | 3 | Value Content | Educate, establish expertise | Internal content link |
| 4 | 5 | Social Proof | Build credibility via results/testimonials | Soft affiliate mention |
| 5 | 7 | Deeper Dive | Pre-sell problem awareness | Resource / affiliate |
| 6 | 10 | Soft Pitch | Introduce offer with context | Affiliate (soft) |
| 7 | 14 | Hard CTA | Close with full value pitch | Affiliate (direct) |
