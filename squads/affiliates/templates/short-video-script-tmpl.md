## Script Structure

| Segment | Time | Purpose | Formula |
|---------|------|---------|---------|
| Hook | 0–3s | Stop the scroll | Pattern interrupt + open loop |
| Problem | 3–8s | Qualify + agitate | "If you [problem], this is for you" |
| Agitate | 8–15s | Deepen pain | Make them feel the cost of NOT solving it |
| Solution | 15–40s | Reveal + teach | The payoff they stayed for |
| CTA | 40–60s | Drive action | One clear next step |

## Filming Notes

- [ ] Hook visual is ready before pressing record
- [ ] Good lighting (ring light, window, or outdoor)
- [ ] Audio: quiet environment or lav mic
- [ ] Eye contact with camera (not screen preview)
- [ ] Re-film hook minimum 3 times — pick the most energetic
- [ ] Caption accuracy review before posting
