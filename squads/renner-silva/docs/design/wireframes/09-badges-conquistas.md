## Behavior Notes

- **Badge grid:** 2 columns on mobile, 3-4 on tablet, 5 on desktop. Gap of 12px.
- **Earned badges:** Full color icon, module-specific glow effect. Rarity influences visual treatment:
  - Common: No border glow, simple colored icon
  - Rare: Subtle shimmer border, `--info` tint
  - Epic: Pulsing glow border, `--accent` tint
  - Legendary: Animated golden glow, `--secondary` tint, star particles
- **Locked badges:** Grayscale icon, lock overlay in center. Name visible but faded. Rarity label visible.
- **Next badge card:** Prominently placed at top showing closest-to-unlock badge with progress bar and action link.
- **Badge earned animation:** When newly earned (within session), badge card plays scale-in + glow animation. "Novo!" pill badge appears for 24h.
- **Filter tabs:** Todos (all), Conquistados (earned only), Proximos (close to earning, >50% progress), Bloqueados (far from earning).
- **Click interaction:** Opens detail modal with large icon, description, criteria, and progress (if locked). Link to relevant module/action.
- **Rarity distribution (15 badges):**
  - Common (4): Primeiro Passo, Explorador, Anotador, Perfeccionista
  - Rare (5): Abertura Criativa, Vendedor Sonhos, Dedicado, Nota 10, Expresso
  - Epic (3): Mestre Estrutura, Contador Historias, Incansavel
  - Legendary (3): Aplauso de Pe, Projeto Final, Metodo Completo
- **Accessibility:** Each badge card has `aria-label` with badge name, status (conquistado/bloqueado), and rarity. Grid uses `role="list"`.
