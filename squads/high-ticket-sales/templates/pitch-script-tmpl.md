# Pitch Script Template

Complete pitch/webinar script following Perfect Webinar (Russell Brunson) + Hormozi frameworks.

**Offer:** [OFFER NAME]
**Created by:** [NAME]
**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]

## Hook / Opening Frame (5-7 minutes)

### Attention Grabber
"[BOLD STATEMENT OR QUESTION THAT STOPS SCROLL]"

Example: "In the next 60 minutes, I'm going to show you how [AVATAR] can [DREAM OUTCOME] without [MAIN OBSTACLE], even if [OBJECTION]."

### Authority + Rapport
"My name is [NAME], and [CREDIBILITY STATEMENT]. I've helped [NUMBER] people [RESULT], including [NOTABLE EXAMPLES]."

**Social Proof:**
- [Testimonial 1 — Quick win or big result]
- [Media mention or credential]

### Frame Setting
"Here's what we're NOT going to do today:
- [Anti-pattern 1]
- [Anti-pattern 2]
- [Anti-pattern 3]

Instead, I'm going to show you [NEW OPPORTUNITY/VEHICLE] that makes [OLD WAY] obsolete."

### Agenda
"By the end of this presentation, you'll know:
1. [Learning outcome 1]
2. [Learning outcome 2]
3. [Learning outcome 3]
4. And I'll show you how to [GET STARTED / APPLY THIS] if you're a fit."

**Disqualification:**
"This isn't for everyone. If you're [WRONG AVATAR TRAIT], this won't work for you. But if you're [RIGHT AVATAR TRAIT], keep watching."

## Teaching Moment: The One Insight (10-15 minutes)

### Reframe Statement
"The reason you haven't [ACHIEVED GOAL] yet isn't because you're [NOT SMART / NOT WORKING HARD / UNLUCKY]. It's because [REFRAME INSIGHT]."

**Example:** "The reason you're struggling to close high-ticket sales isn't because you're not good at sales. It's because you're using low-ticket tactics for high-ticket offers."

### The Big Idea
"What if I told you that [COUNTERINTUITIVE STATEMENT]?"

**Proof of Concept:**
- [Data point or study]
- [Personal story demonstrating insight]
- [Industry shift or trend]

### Aha Moment
"Once you understand [INSIGHT], everything changes. [STORY OF TRANSFORMATION]."

## Secret 2: Internal Belief (They CAN Do It) (8-10 minutes)

### False Belief Breakdown
"You might be thinking, 'This sounds great, but it won't work for me because [COMMON INTERNAL OBJECTION].'"

**Examples:**
- "I'm not smart enough"
- "I don't have the skills"
- "I've failed before"

### Epiphany Story
"I used to believe that too. Let me tell you about [STUDENT/CLIENT NAME]..."

[Tell story of someone who overcame the same internal belief and succeeded]

**Key Beats:**
1. [Student] believed they couldn't do it
2. They tried [VEHICLE] anyway
3. They discovered [TRUTH]
4. Now they [RESULT]

### Universal Truth
"The truth is, [EMPOWERING BELIEF]. You don't need [FALSE REQUIREMENT]. You just need [SIMPLE REQUIREMENT]."

**Social Proof:**
- [Testimonial 3 — "I didn't think I could do it, but..."]

---

## Secret 3: External Belief (Nothing Stops Them) (8-10 minutes)

### False External Beliefs
"You might also be thinking, '[EXTERNAL OBJECTION].'"

**Examples:**
- "I don't have time"
- "The market is too saturated"
- "The economy is bad"
- "My situation is different"

### Epiphany Story
"I hear this all the time. Let me tell you about [STUDENT/CLIENT NAME]..."

[Tell story of someone who overcame the same external obstacle and succeeded]

**Key Beats:**
1. [Student] had [EXTERNAL OBSTACLE]
2. They used [STRATEGY/TOOL FROM YOUR OFFER]
3. They overcame [OBSTACLE]
4. Now they [RESULT]

### Universal Truth
"The truth is, [EXTERNAL OBSTACLE] is not the real problem. The real problem is [REFRAME]. And once you [SOLUTION], [OBSTACLE] disappears."

**Social Proof:**
- [Testimonial 4 — "Even though I [OBSTACLE], I still got [RESULT]"]

## Price Reveal (2-3 minutes)

### Anchor to Higher Price
"Now, if I were to charge what this is really worth, it would be $[HIGH PRICE]. And honestly, if you got even [FRACTION] of the results my clients get, it would be worth [10X PRICE]."

### Discount Reasoning
"But here's the thing: [REASON FOR LOWER PRICE — e.g., building case studies, new launch, celebrating milestone, etc.]"

### Price Reveal
"So you're not going to pay $[HIGH PRICE]. You're not even going to pay $[MID PRICE]. Today, you can get everything for just $[ACTUAL PRICE]."

### Payment Plans (If Applicable)
"And because I want to make this a no-brainer, I'm offering payment plans:
- Pay in full: $[AMOUNT] (save $[SAVINGS])
- [X] payments of $[AMOUNT]"

## Guarantee (2-3 minutes)

### Guarantee Statement
"And just so you have no risk at all, I'm giving you my [TYPE] guarantee:"

"[GUARANTEE DESCRIPTION — e.g., 'If you implement what I teach and don't see [RESULT] within [TIMEFRAME], I'll refund every penny and let you keep all the bonuses.']"

### Why This Guarantee Works
"I can offer this because [CONFIDENCE IN PRODUCT]. The only way you lose is if you don't take action."

## CTA + Urgency (3-5 minutes)

### Primary CTA
"So here's what I want you to do right now:
1. Click the button below
2. Fill out the form
3. [NEXT STEP — checkout, application, call booking]"

**Button Text:** "[RESERVE YOUR SPOT / APPLY NOW / GET INSTANT ACCESS]"

### Urgency Reminder
"Now, I need to tell you something important: [SCARCITY/URGENCY EXPLANATION]."

**Examples:**
- "We only have [NUMBER] spots available, and we're already [PERCENTAGE] sold out."
- "This offer closes on [DATE] at [TIME]."
- "The bonuses expire at midnight."

### Fear of Missing Out (FOMO)
"If you're on the fence, ask yourself: What will your life look like 6 months from now if you DON'T do this?"

"Compare that to what it could look like if you DO: [PAINT PICTURE OF TRANSFORMATION]."

### Final Push
"The link is below. Click it now before [URGENCY TRIGGER]."

---

## FAQ / Objection Pre-Handling (5-8 minutes)

### Q: "How is this different from [competitor/previous solution]?"
**A:** [Unique mechanism, new opportunity, category of one]

### Q: "How much time will this take?"
**A:** [Realistic time commitment + effort reduction]

### Q: "What if it doesn't work for me?"
**A:** [Point to guarantee]

### Q: "I've tried [similar thing] before and it didn't work."
**A:** [Differentiation + new mechanism]

### Q: "I need to think about it / talk to my spouse."
**A:** "I totally get that. But here's the thing: [URGENCY REINFORCEMENT]. Also, the guarantee means there's zero risk. The only risk is doing nothing and staying stuck."

### Q: "Is this a scam / too good to be true?"
**A:** "I understand skepticism. I was skeptical too. But look at [SOCIAL PROOF]. The guarantee protects you. The only way to know for sure is to try."

### Q: "Can I get a discount?"
**A:** "The price is already discounted from $[HIGHER PRICE] to $[CURRENT PRICE]. Plus, [PAYMENT PLANS]. This is the lowest it will ever be."

## Final Close (2-3 minutes)

### Emotional Close
"Look, I can't make this decision for you. But I can tell you this: [FUTURE PACING — where they'll be if they say yes vs. where they'll be if they say no]."

"You have two paths:
1. **Path A:** You do nothing. Six months from now, you're still [STUCK IN SAME PLACE].
2. **Path B:** You take action today. Six months from now, you're [LIVING DREAM OUTCOME]."

### Logical Close
"And with the [GUARANTEE], you have nothing to lose. The only risk is doing nothing."

### Final CTA
"Click the button below right now. I'll see you on the inside."

**Button Text:** "[CTA TEXT]"

## Post-Pitch Follow-Up Sequence

### Immediately After (Live Webinar)
- "Link is in the chat. Click it now."
- Answer questions in Q&A
- Remind of urgency every 10 minutes

### Email 1 (Immediately After)
**Subject:** "Here's the link (don't wait)"
**Body:** Recap of offer + CTA + urgency

### Email 2 (T-24h)
**Subject:** "Only 24 hours left"
**Body:** Restate dream outcome + objection handling + CTA

### Email 3 (T-6h)
**Subject:** "6 hours left — last call"
**Body:** Final urgency push + FOMO

### Email 4 (T-1h)
**Subject:** "Final hour — doors closing"
**Body:** Countdown + emotional close

### Email 5 (After Close)
**Subject:** "We're closed (but...)"
**Body:** Optional waitlist or future opportunity
