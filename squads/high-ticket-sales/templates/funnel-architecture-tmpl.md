## Funnel Stage 3: Core Offer (Middle of Value Ladder)

### Offer
**Name:** [CORE OFFER NAME]
**Price:** $[500-2000]
**Format:** [Course / Coaching Program / Mastermind / Software / Service]

### Launch Strategy
**Method:** [Evergreen Webinar / Live Webinar / VSL / Email Sequence / Challenge / PLF]

### Launch Sequence (Choose One)

#### Option A: Webinar Funnel
1. **Registration Page:** Opt-in for webinar
2. **Confirmation Sequence:** 3-5 emails (show up reminders + value)
3. **Webinar:** 60-90 min (Hook → Teach → Stack → Close)
4. **Replay Sequence:** 3-5 emails (limited replay, urgency)
5. **Close:** Cart closes 3-7 days after webinar

#### Option B: VSL Funnel
1. **Landing Page:** Video + CTA
2. **Application Form:** Qualify prospects
3. **Call Booking:** Schedule sales call
4. **Call Script:** Close on call (see sales-call-tmpl.md)

#### Option C: Challenge Funnel
1. **Challenge Registration:** 5-day free challenge
2. **Daily Content:** Teach + build relationship
3. **Day 5:** Pitch core offer
4. **Close:** Cart open for 3-5 days post-challenge

#### Option D: Product Launch Formula (PLF)
1. **Pre-Launch:** 3 videos (Opportunity, Transformation, Ownership)
2. **Launch Day:** Cart opens, webinar, or live event
3. **Open Cart:** 5-7 days, daily emails
4. **Close Cart:** Final urgency push

### Email Sequences

#### Soap Opera Sequence (Pre-Launch — 5 emails)
**Day 1:** Set the stage (introduce story)
**Day 2:** Build tension (reveal problem)
**Day 3:** Climax (discovery of solution)
**Day 4:** Resolution (show results)
**Day 5:** CTA (offer revealed)

#### Daily Seinfeld Sequence (Post-Launch — Ongoing)
- Daily emails with stories, lessons, or content
- Occasional soft pitches to core or high-ticket offers
- Keep list warm and engaged

## Funnel Stage 4: High-Ticket (Backend Offer)

### Offer
**Name:** [HIGH-TICKET OFFER NAME]
**Price:** $[5K-50K]
**Format:** [1-on-1 Coaching / Mastermind / Done-For-You / Intensive / Consulting]

### Qualification Process
1. **Application Form:** Screen for fit (budget, commitment, goals)
2. **Pre-Qualification:** Review application, disqualify if not a fit
3. **Sales Call:** 30-60 min (see sales-call-tmpl.md)
4. **Close:** Binary choice close (Plan A or Plan B)

### Promotion Strategy
- **Who Sees It:** Only buyers of core offer OR high-intent leads
- **When:** 30-90 days after core offer purchase OR during onboarding
- **How:** Email invitation, application funnel, or direct outreach

### Application Form Questions
1. What's your current situation?
2. What's your biggest challenge right now?
3. What have you tried before?
4. What's your goal for the next 90 days?
5. Why do you think this program will help you?
6. What's your budget for solving this problem?
7. Are you ready to invest and commit if this is a fit?

**Target Conversion Rate:** [NUMBER]% of core offer buyers
