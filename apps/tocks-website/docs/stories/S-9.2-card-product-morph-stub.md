# Story S-9.2 — Card → Product Morph Animation

## Scope

Aplicar CSS custom contract ao morph cross-route: `share="morph"` prop → `::view-transition-group(.morph)` com `animation-duration: 400ms` + `::view-transition-image-pair(.morph)` via-blur keyframe 3px mid-flight. Adicionar directional tags `transitionTypes={['nav-forward']}` em product-card Link AND `['nav-back']` no "← Coleção" back-link do product layout. Sem scene content slide (morph é star; directional só soft-anchor).

1. **AC-1** — `<ViewTransition>` wrapping de S-9.1 recebe `share="morph"` prop em ambas as rotas.
